Prof. Simon Hanslmayr, PI
Simon is a full professor at the Centre for Cognitive Neuroimaging at the University of Glasgow. He is the director of the Cognition and Oscillations Lab where he investigates the role of brain oscillations for human cogntion. Simon studies Psychology at the University of Salzburg and did his PhD in 2005 under supervision of Prof. Wolfgang Klimesch.
